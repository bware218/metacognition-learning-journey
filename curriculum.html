Introduction to Metacognition

Learning Objectives

  • Define metacognition and understand its three core components

  • Complete your first metacognitive self-assessment

Key Concepts

What you know about your own thinking

Metacognitive Regulation

How you control and monitor your thinking

Metacognitive Experiences

The feelings and judgments during thinking

This Week's Activities

  • What are my current strengths and blind spots as a thinker?
  • How might developing metacognitive skills benefit my academic and personal goals?
